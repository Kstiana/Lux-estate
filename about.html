Our Story

Fifteen Years Of Knowing Nigeria's Best Addresses

LuxEstate was founded in 2011 by a small team of Lagos-based advisors who were tired of watching serious buyers get burned by unverified listings and vanishing agents. The idea was simple: build a firm that treats every transaction with the same care as a first home purchase, whether the property costs ten million naira or ten billion.

Fifteen years later, that principle has not changed. We have grown from a single desk in Ikoyi to a full advisory team spanning Lagos, Abuja, and Port Harcourt, but every listing that reaches our catalogue still passes through the same title verification and site inspection process our founders insisted on from day one.

Our Mission

To make luxury real estate in Nigeria transparent, well-documented, and stress-free, so that every buyer, tenant, and investor can make decisions with complete confidence in what they are signing.

Our Vision

To be the first name that comes to mind whenever a serious buyer or investor thinks about premium property anywhere in West Africa, known as much for our integrity as for the addresses we represent.
